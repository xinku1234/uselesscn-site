Browser art toy FAQ

What counts as a browser art toy?

A browser art toy is a playful web page for making visuals, sounds, drawings, patterns, or small interactive scenes without installing an app.

Are these useful for artists?

Some can inspire colors or motion ideas, but the main value is low-pressure play. They are intentionally more delightful than professional.
