Are pointless websites the same as weird websites?

They overlap. Pointless websites usually emphasize a single unnecessary action, while weird websites can include stranger art, games, or internet experiments.

What if a classic link is blocked by bots?

Bot-blocked does not always mean dead. UselessCN treats those cases carefully and does not call a site broken unless access problems are clear or persistent.
