About UselessCN

A small doorway into the playful web.

UselessCN collects weird websites, pointless browser toys, tiny games, odd visual experiments and calming distractions. The point is not to make you more productive. The point is to give your brain a small, harmless detour.

What is it?

UselessCN is a one-button portal and curated directory for the strange, funny and beautifully unnecessary parts of the internet. Click the button when you are bored, tired, creatively stuck or simply curious.

Who is it for?

It is for bored office workers, students taking a quick break, makers looking for odd interaction ideas, designers collecting playful references and anyone who misses the old feeling that the web could be surprising.

Why does it exist?

Most modern websites push you to buy, optimize, scroll forever or become more efficient. UselessCN is intentionally smaller: a tiny escape from endless feeds, productivity pressure and overly serious internet routines.

How is it maintained?

The collection is checked regularly for dead links, bot-blocked pages and better category coverage. New long-tail pages organize the collection by mood and use case: quick boredom breaks, creative browser toys, weird visuals, tiny games and oddly satisfying sites.

The promise

One click should take you somewhere that feels at least one of these things: funny, strange, calm, surprising, visually interesting or creatively inspiring.
